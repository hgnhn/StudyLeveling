About StudyLeveling

StudyLeveling is our passion project, created to digitize the learning environment. We provide a secure, private space where teachers can manage students and assign work, while students can track their progress anytime, anywhere. With our unique features, learners can feel comfortable and even entertained right in their educational space!

Unique Features

⚔️

Integrated Minigames!

StudyLeveling features a built-in roguelike minigame system, allowing users to learn and play at the same time, relieving academic pressure.

🌟

Leveling System

Earn experience points with every assignment. Leveling up not only makes you stronger but also unlocks awesome characters, avatars, and exclusive titles!

🔥

Study Streaks

Log in and learn every day to keep your study streak alive!

📚

Share & Connect

Students don't just receive materials from teachers—they can also upload, share notes, and connect with peers in their classroom.

📜

Classrooms

Teachers can easily create classes, post announcements, manage student enrollment, and assign homework or exams.

🏆

Leaderboards

Compete for the top spot based on streaks, test scores, assignments completed, and community contributions.
